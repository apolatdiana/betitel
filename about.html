About Us

Betitel Networks (U) Limited is a leading ICT company based in Uganda, serving clients across East Africa. We specialize in cutting-edge technology solutions, providing high- quality services tailored to meet the diverse needs of businesses in the region.

Brief History

Established in December 2019 and incorporated in May 2022, Betitel Networks (U) Limited has rapidly grown to become a trusted provider of innovative ICT solutions in East Africa.

Mission Statement:

To connect East Africa through innovative telecommunications and ICT solutions, enhancing connectivity, driving economic growth, and enriching lives.

Vision:

To be the leading provider of telecommunications and ICT services in East Africa, pioneering technological advancements and empowering communities.
